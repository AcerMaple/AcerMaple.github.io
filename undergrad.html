Undergraduate Tutoring

Dr. Corsten Douglas completed a PhD in Biological sciences (Biochemistry) under the supervision of Professor Sir John Walker FRS at the MRC mitochondrial biology unit. Corsten's thesis, "The assembly pathways of human ATP synthase" showed the requirement of the supernumerary subunits e, f and g in the assembly of the FO domain of ATP synthase.

Corsten specialises in the subject of bioenergetics and mitochondria, with an emphasis on ATP synthase, however, most undergraduate biology and biochemistry subject areas can be catered for.

Douglas Tutoring offers 1:1 tutoring to undergraduates studying natural sciences (biology and biochemistry). Areas covered include:

  • Methods for effective reading and analysis of research journal articles
  • Help with scientific writing and tutorials
  • Study skills and time management
  • Revision and exam practice
