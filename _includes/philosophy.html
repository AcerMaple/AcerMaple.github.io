I aim to encourage as many students as possible to enjoy and pursue science subjects, such as biology, chemistry, physics and medicine. Having a firm grounding in these logic-based subjects provides a wide range of transferable skills that can be used in almost any career.

In order for students to excel in their exams, and then at work after their education, Douglas Tutoring aims to increase awareness and engagement in:

  • Time management
  • Planning
  • Working as a team
  • Lone working
  • Keeping motivated during difficult projects

Students with these skills will be able to excel in almost any career, not just in science based jobs.
