Corsten's teaching style

I will provide structured 1:1 support to supplement lessons taught in school in order to increase grades, confidence in, and enjoyment of science and maths subjects. While I am an expert in science subjects, I am also able to cater for other subjects (Link to English, ICT, PE).

Every one of us is individual, and this individuality extends to our learning, memory and recall. 1:1 private tuition provided by Douglas Tutoring aims to identify the individual strengths and weaknesses in the learning, memory and recall of a student, so that these can be utilised or worked on in a calm, kind and encouraging manner.

Learning styles will be used to supplement individual learning needs. Getting a student to think about their learning is what ultimately improves learning, memory and recall, rather than catering for individual learning styles. The use of all four common learning styles together allows the student to focus on their learning, which is currently thought to improve the students understanding, memory and recall.

No matter what learning style may be suited or preferred by each individual student, the students all have one thing in common: they all need to be able to sit down and pass exams. This is where the learning style needs to be adapted to the mainly linear format of exams and assignments: reading (and understanding the question) and writing (the correct answers).

Students who think of themselves as linear (reading and writing) learners will be most suited to this, but those who identify as active, auditory or visual learners will be supported by Corsten to link their learning style(s) to the format of their exams.
