Olivia Guest

Hi, I am a cognitive computational neuroscientist currently carrying out postdoctoral research in the Department of Experimental Psychology at the University of Oxford.

I have a Software Sustainability Institute fellowship for 2016. I also am an associate editor at ReScience.

I am available for consultation services regarding computational cognitive modelling.
